What it does

Everything the counter needs. Nothing it doesn’t.

DukanPro strips the shop down to the moves you make two hundred times a day — and makes each one fast, forgiving, and one‑handed.

Speed

A cash sale in seconds.

Three taps from home — pick the item, it fills the price, SAVE. Cash or credit, one item or ten, the register keeps pace with the queue. The whole app is tuned to the flow of a busy counter, not a spreadsheet.

Receive

Snap the bono.

Photograph a supplier’s delivery note; DukanPro reads the lines and turns them into stock. Glance, fix anything wrong, save.

Online & offline

Seamless, signal or none.

Sell, receive and take payments whether you’re connected or not — every action lands instantly on the phone and syncs in the background when you’re back, sparing your data. One experience, online or off; the shop never waits on the network.

Money

Every debt, both ways.

Who owes you, who you owe, what sold today, what’s running low — one calm home screen you can read at a glance.

Scan

Scan or type — your call.

Barcode scanning with the camera or a cheap Bluetooth scanner, backed by a local catalogue so a scan resolves instantly, even offline.

Sign‑in

Sign in with WhatsApp — no passwords to forget.

A one‑time code arrives on WhatsApp, the app most shopkeepers already live in. No SIM swaps, no forgotten passwords, no support call. Just a number and a code.
